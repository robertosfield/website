Detailed Description

RenderGraph encapsulates the vkCmdBeginRenderPass/vkCmdEndRenderPass functionality. Member variables of the RenderGraph map to the settings of the VkRenderPassBeginInfo. During the RecordTraversal children of RenderGraph are visited within the vkCmdBeginRenderPass/vkCmdEndRenderPass pair.
